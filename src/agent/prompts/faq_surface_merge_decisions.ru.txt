STAGE E — Merge decisions.

Верни merge_decisions[] с полями:
survivor_surface_key, merged_surface_keys, keep_separate_surface_keys, decision_type, reason, confidence.

Правила:
- duplicates -> decision_type merge.
- umbrella_contains и specializes -> keep_separate.
- overlaps -> keep_separate, кроме случая полностью одинакового user intent.
- Telegram и web-widget keep_separate.
- manual merge и hide/archive keep_separate.
- product overview и поисковая поверхность keep_separate.
- Не удаляй source refs и не теряй ownership при merge decision.
